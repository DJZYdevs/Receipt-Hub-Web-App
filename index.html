Archive & storage

      
    

    

      
Receipts are saved to this browser's local storage (IndexedDB), so closing the app or restarting your phone won't lose anything — it'll be exactly as you left it next time you open this file. This is device-local, not a cloud account, so it won't sync across phones/browsers on its own.


      
Google Drive archive

      
A Drive folder is ready to receive your receipts: Engineer of Things — Receipts, with Personal / Work / Investments / Other subfolders matching the app.

      Open Drive folder ↗
      
This static file can't upload to Drive directly from your phone (no backend to hold the sign-in), so for now: tap Export receipts below, then share the downloaded file to the Drive app's folder above. A direct in-app upload would need a small backend — let me know if you want that built next.

      

      
What gets archived per receipt

      
Original image(s), extracted fields, your verified/edited values, timestamp of verification, and folder/category — so every entry is fully auditable later.


      
OCR engine (text extraction)

      
This prototype uses OCR.space — a real, free OCR API (25,000 requests/month free tier). It's currently running on the shared public demo key, which is rate-limited and shared with other developers testing the same API, so you may occasionally see a "rate limit" error if a lot of people are testing it at once.

      

        
        
      

      
Get your own free key in 30 seconds at ocr.space/ocrapi (no credit card) and paste it above — it's saved in this browser only and unlocks much higher limits.


      
Known merchants

      
Every time you verify a receipt, the app remembers the establishment name and category. Next time that merchant's name appears anywhere in the scanned text — even on a messy or oddly-formatted receipt — it's matched instantly instead of guessed.

      


      
Reset data

      
This clears every receipt saved on this device. Export first if you want a backup — this cannot be undone.
