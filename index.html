        Account
        

          
Logged in as admin. Each account keeps its own separate receipts on this device — remember this is a testing-only gate, not real account security.

          
          
Receipts are saved to this browser's local storage (IndexedDB), so closing the app or restarting your phone won't lose anything. This is device-local, not a cloud account.

        

      


      

        Export & Import
        

          
Build a report of your receipts, or bring in data from elsewhere.

Import

          
Bring receipts in from a spreadsheet (reliable), a PDF report (best-effort — every imported row will need verification, same as a scanned receipt), or a full export zip (report + photos, matched automatically by filename).

          

            
            
          

          
          
          
          
        

      


      

        Google Drive archive
        

          
A Drive folder is ready to receive your receipts: Engineer of Things — Receipts, with Personal / Work / Investments / Other subfolders matching the app.

          Open Drive folder ↗
          
This static file can't upload to Drive directly from your phone (no backend to hold the sign-in), so for now: export a report or zip above, then share the download to the Drive app's folder above.

        

      


      

        OCR engine (text extraction)
        

          
This prototype uses OCR.space — a real, free OCR API (25,000 requests/month free tier). It's currently running on the shared public demo key, which is rate-limited and shared with other developers testing the same API.

          

            
            
          

          
Get your own free key in 30 seconds at ocr.space/ocrapi (no credit card) and paste it above.

        

      


      

        Known merchants
        

          
Every time you verify a receipt, the app remembers the establishment name and category. Next time that merchant's name appears anywhere in the scanned text, it's matched instantly instead of guessed.

          

        

      


      

        Categories
        

          
Manage your category list here anytime — separate from the quick "+ Add new category" shortcut available while verifying a receipt.

          

          
        

      


      

        Reset data
        

          
This clears every receipt saved on this device. Export first if you want a backup — this cannot be undone.
